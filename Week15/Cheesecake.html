    • Old Fashioned Cheesecake

    Ingredients:

    • 1 and 1/2 cups finely crumbled graham crackers
    • 6 tablespoons melted butter
    • 1 pound cream sugar
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 1 tablespoon Vanilla Bean Paste
    • 1 and 1/4 cups Sugar
    • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
    • 3 Large eggs
    • 1 cup Sour Cream
    • 1/8 teaspoon Salt

    Preparation Instructions:

    1. Prepare graham cracker crust - mix together with a fork until all Ingredients are moistened.
    2. Spread the mixture evenly in 10 inch pan. Using your fingertips or the flat bottom of a drinking glass
    3. firmly press the mixture over the bottom and 1/2 inch up the sides of a pan. Freeze for 20 minutes.
    4. For a crunchier crust bake in a preheated 350 degree oven until lightly browned, 10 to 15 minutes instead of freezing it.
    5. Preheat oven to 300 degrees. In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese until creamy, about 30 seconds. Do not over beat.
    6. Gradually beat in a 1 cup sugar and vanilla extract. Beat in 1 egg at a time, just until incorporated,
    7. scraping the sides of the bowl and the beater after each addition.
    8. Scrape the batter into the crust and smooth the top. Place on a cookie sheet.
    9. Bake until the center just barely jiggles when the pan is tapped, 45 to 55 minutes.
    10. Turn off the oven, prop the door ajar with a folded hot pad or the handle of a wooden spoon and let the cake cool in the oven for 1/2 an hour.
    11. Remove from the oven and continue to cool in the pan on a rack for at least 1 hour.
    12. Combine sour cream, 1/4 cup sugar, vanilla bean paste and salt in a small bowl.
    13. Spread over the cake. Let cool completely in the pan.
    14. Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours preferable 24 hours, before serving.

    Prep Time Additional Time total Time
    15 Mins 30 Mins 45 Mins
