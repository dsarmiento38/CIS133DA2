    • Cherry Empanadas Recipe

    Ingredients:

      Cherry Filling
    • 2 cup fresh cherries, coarsely chopped
    • ½ cup sugar
    • 2 tablespoons water
    • 2 tablespoon cornstarch

      • Cream Cheese Filling
    • 8 ounce cream cheese
    • ½ cup sugar
    • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

      • Egg Wash
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tablespoon water

    Baking Instructions

    Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
    2. In a small saucepan over low heat, add cherries and sugar. Stir
    3. Mix water and cornstarch together until combined and add to the cherry mixture.
    4. Cook on low for 5-7 minutes or until the cherry mixture has thickened. Set aside to cool.
    Cream Cheese Filling
    1. In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.
    2. Add sugar, lemon juice and vanilla extract. Beat until combined.
    3. Spoon a spoonful of cream cheese mixture in the center of the empanada dough followed by a spoonful of cherry mixture..
      Fold the dough over in half and moisten the edges.
    4. Using a fork, crimp the edges to secure.
    Egg Wash
      Mix egg and water together in a small bowl and brush over each empanadas. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
